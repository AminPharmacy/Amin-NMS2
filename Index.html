How is your new medication?

Your pharmacy is checking in to see how you are getting on. Please take a few minutes to answer these questions honestly — your responses help us make sure you are safe and well supported.

🔒

The information you provide is used solely to support your care and to help us make sure your new medication is working well for you. It will be stored securely within the pharmacy as part of your health record and used for no other purpose. If we have any concerns based on your answers, we will give you a call or liaise with your GP to help resolve any issues. If you feel seriously unwell at any point, please call 111 or 999.
